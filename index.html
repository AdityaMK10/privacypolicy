Privacy Policy for Screen Time Limiter Extension

Effective Date: 19th January 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how the Screen Time Limiter Chrome extension collects, uses, and protects your information. By using this extension, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Data Collection and Use

The extension temporarily uses the hostname of visited websites to track and enforce time limits set by the user. This data is only used within the browser and is not shared, stored permanently, or transmitted to external servers.

2. Data Storage

All data is stored locally in the browser's storage. The extension does not store or share any personal information, and no data is transmitted outside the user's device.

3. User Privacy

We do not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information, health information, financial data, authentication information, personal communications, location data, or web history outside the scope of the extension's functionality.

4. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected here, and users will be notified accordingly.
